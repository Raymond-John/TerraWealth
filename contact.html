FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about investing with TerraWealth Agro.

How do I start investing?

Simply create an account, verify your identity, fund your wallet, and browse available projects. You can start with as little as $250.

What is the minimum investment?

Minimum investments vary by project, starting from $250 for agriculture projects and $2,000 for real estate.

How are returns paid?

Returns are credited directly to your wallet at the end of the investment period. You can withdraw or reinvest as you choose.

Is my investment secure?

All investments carry risk. We conduct thorough due diligence and structure investments with appropriate safeguards, but returns are not guaranteed.

Can I withdraw early?

Investments are typically locked for the project duration. Early withdrawal may be possible in certain circumstances but is not guaranteed.

What fees do you charge?

We charge a small management fee on returns (typically 5-10%). There are no hidden fees or charges for account maintenance.

Important Notice: Returns are not guaranteed. All investments carry risk, including the potential loss of capital. Past performance does not guarantee future results. Please read our risk disclosure and consult with a financial advisor before investing.
